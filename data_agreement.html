Data Usage Statement and Agreement

Welcome to the Stable diffusion WebUI for Human-AI interaction study. Before you proceed, please take a moment to read and understand how we collect, use, and share data provided by users like you.

1. Data Collection

By using our web app, you agree to allow us to collect specific data related to your usage patterns, preferences, and interactions within the app. This may include:

• The prompts and negative prompts you use for generating images.

• The pictures you have generated.

• Your responses to the generations, including the satisfaction scores and comments.

• The information of models for generating your pictures.

2. Data Usage

We primarily use the data we collect to:

• Analyze how the Human guide AI improves the painting qualities in words.

• Analyze how the AI processes the information from Humans to improve.

• Analyze the relationship between the painting changes and the individual demographics.

3. Data Sharing & Meta-Analysis

We may aggregate and anonymize the data for meta-analyses. These analyses will not include personal identifiers, ensuring the privacy of individual users. Results from these meta-analyses may be:

• Shared with the University of Michigan for research or academic purposes.

• Published in reports, publications, or other public formats.

Please note, we will never sell your individual data or use it for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Opting Out & Control

You have control over your data:

• If you wish to opt out of specific data collection practices or want to know more about the data we've collected from you, please contact us at gyuanfan@umich.edu.

• You can delete your account or data at any time, with all associated data being removed from our servers.

By continuing to use Stable diffusion WebUI for Human-AI interaction study, you signify your acceptance of this Data Usage Statement. If you do not agree to this statement, please refrain from using our web app.
